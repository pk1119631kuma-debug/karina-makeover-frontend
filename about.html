The Story of Karina Makeover

Welcome to Karina Makeover, where beauty meets artistry. Founded in 2016, we've been serving the beauty needs of Mumbai's most discerning clients for over 8 years.

At the heart of our business is a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel beautiful and confident. Our mission is to enhance natural beauty and create unforgettable moments for our clients.

We specialize in luxury makeup services for all occasions - from intimate bridal moments to grand celebrations. Each makeup application is a unique art piece, tailored to bring out your best self.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment through our website using the Book Now button or by calling us directly at +91 98765 43210.

Do you offer trial makeup?

Yes! We offer trial sessions. Contact us to schedule a consultation and trial makeup at a convenient time.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours before the appointment are free. Late cancellations may incur charges.

Do you provide makeup for home events?

Absolutely! We provide on-site makeup services for home events, venues, and studios. Travel charges may apply.

What brands do you use?

We use top international brands like MAC, Charlotte Tilbury, NARS, and Indian brands like Lakme for premium quality.

How long does makeup typically last?

Our makeup typically lasts 8-12 hours depending on the occasion and skin type. Bridal makeup is specifically designed to last the entire day and night.
